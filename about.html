About Me

I am Amritbalaji Kaliaperumal, a multifaceted individual with a profound passion for music and innovation. Holding a degree in sound engineering, I have honed my skills as a musician, multi-instrumentalist, singer, rapper, arranger, rhythm programmer, orchestrator, mixing engineer, and mastering engineer. Beyond music, I also possess a background in mechatronics engineering, which has driven me to establish Zara Technology. With a musical journey that commenced at the tender age of 4, I have dedicated over 16 years to nurturing my talents and expertise. My unwavering commitment to pushing artistic boundaries and embracing technological advancements fuels my drive to craft immersive musical experiences and contribute meaningfully to the world of sound and innovation.
